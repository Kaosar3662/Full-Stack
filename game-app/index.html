How to play

  1. Press Start Game to begin a 30 second round.
  2. Click the glowing mole as quickly as you can to score points.
  3. Missing the mole or clicking an empty hole lowers your streak.
  4. Keep an eye on the timer and chase a new personal best!

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Refresh the page for a new challenge!
